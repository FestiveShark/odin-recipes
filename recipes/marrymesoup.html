Marry Me Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

The concept of “Marry Me Chicken” has become somewhat of a phenomenon. The idea of a chicken so tasty, you want to marry the person preparing it for you is quite the attractive prospect, after all. The chicken in this recipe is not quite the showstealer that the original Marry Me Chicken was, but the soup can no doubt match it.

This recipe van serve six people, and should result in a lovely, creamy soup that goes down very easily. The recipe calls for a sizeable amount of spice, which can be increased or decreased to taste. Definitely taste the soup while seasoning! A healthy serving of vegetables ensures you'll leave the dinner table having had a balanced meal.

Ingredients

For six people

Soup base and vegetables

  • 500g cooked chicken
  • 150g pasta of choice
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, finely cut
  • 2 celery stalks, finely cut
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely cut
  • 60g fresh spinach
  • 170g tomato paste
  • 40g sun-dried tomatoes, finely cut

Fluids & dairy

  • 1L chicken broth
  • 700ml water
  • 240ml heavy cream
  • 60g cream cheese
  • 25g grated parmesan cheese

Spices & tastemakers

  • 1 tsp Cajun spices
  • 1 tsp Italian spices
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp oil (from the sun-dried tomatoes)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Total 55 minutes of prep time

  1. Ensure your chicken is cooked. If not, bring a pot of water to boil and cook the chicken appropriately. Pull the boiled chicken using a pair of forks.
  2. Add the olive oil, sun-dried tomato oil, onion, carrot and celery to a different pot with high sides and cook for 4-5 minutes over medium heat while stirring occasionally
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 2-3 minutes
  4. Add the garlic and sun-dried tomatoes and cook for another minute
  5. Add the chicken broth, water, heavy cream, Italian seasoning and Cajun seasoning. Bring to a boil and resume the heat to medium low. Let simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Stir in the cooked chicken and pasta and cook for 10 minutes, or until the pasta is properly cooked.
  7. Add the remaining ingredients and cook for one minute, or until the cream cheese melts completely
  8. Season with salt and pepper
